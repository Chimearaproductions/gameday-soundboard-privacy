Privacy Policy for GameDay Soundboard

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

GameDay Soundboard ("we," "us," or "our") built the GameDay Soundboard app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by us at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to external servers.

To provide specific features, the app requires certain device permissions. Here is how they are used:

Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): This permission is used solely for the user-initiated "Voice-Over" feature. This allows users to record their own voice to layer over music tracks. All audio recordings are stored locally within the app's private sandbox on your device. We do not upload these recordings to any servers.

Media/Storage Access: The app requires access to your device's internal storage to allow you to import your own MP3 files for music and SFX. Imported files are copied into the app's local sandbox for consistent playback.

2. Data Storage and Deletion

All data created or imported within the app (player configurations, trimmed audio clips, voice-overs, and images) is stored strictly on the user's device.

User Control: You can delete specific audio tracks or images within the app at any time.

Uninstallation: If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data and recordings associated with the app will be permanently deleted by the Android operating system.

3. Information Sharing

Since we do not collect any information, we do not share, sell, or trade any data with third parties.

4. Security

We value your trust in using our service. Since your data never leaves your device, its security is maintained by your device's native security features.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us
